Our Story
03

Our Story - Transformation with Tea

In a world that is increasingly conscious of the need for businesses to have a purpose beyond profit, YOR Tea stands as a shining example of what is possible when a company combines sustainability, social responsibility, and quality. Apart from bringing the finest tea, we are also empowering rural communities and providing individuals from across India a chance to live a dignified life.

So the next time you sip on a cup of YOR Tea, remember that you are not just enjoying a delicious blend of flavours; you are also contributing to a movement that empowers individuals, uplifts communities, and creates a sustainable future.
